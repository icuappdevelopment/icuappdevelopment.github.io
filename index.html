Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ultimate Blackjack Trainer?

Ultimate Blackjack Trainer is a free Android app designed to help you master blackjack card counting, basic strategy, and advanced deviations through interactive drills and a live simulator.

Can I learn card counting with this app?

Yes! The app features guided HiLo counting drills, true count practice, and speed challenges to help you learn and apply card counting techniques.

Is Ultimate Blackjack Trainer suitable for beginners?

Absolutely. The app includes illustrated introductions and step-by-step modules for both new and experienced players.

Does the app work offline?

Yes, Ultimate Blackjack Trainer is fully functional offline and does not require an internet connection.

Is my data private?

Your data stays on your device. The app is ad-free and privacy-focused.
